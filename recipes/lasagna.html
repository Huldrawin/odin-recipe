The Yummiest Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and a lot of cheese. Filling and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • your favorite lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese and mozzarella cheese, sliced

Steps for making your lasagna

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in tomato sauce.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. To assemble, spread the meat sauce, the ricotta and the mozzarella cheese, make layers using the lasagna noodles.
  5. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!!
